# **Live Betting Lines in NCAA Basketball: Key Predictors and Modeling Insights**

In live **NCAA men’s basketball** betting, oddsmakers adjust lines (spreads and over/under totals) based on a mix of real-time game developments, statistical models, and market dynamics. **Key factors include the game’s current state (score margin and time), pace and efficiency indicators, player availability (injuries/foul trouble), and betting market pressure.** Below we break down the crucial in-game **predictors** bookmakers monitor, and address **modeling strategies** (Random Forest vs. alternative algorithms and ensemble approaches) for forecasting second-half totals and final outcomes. We also cite **verified sources** – from sportsbook analysis to academic research – to illuminate how the industry sets and refines live odds.
